# melo. 🎵
A clean, minimal YouTube Music client.
Your YouTube Music. Beautifully reimagined.

Sign in with Google, search anything on YouTube, manage your queue, like songs, and enjoy dynamic album art theming.

## Features

Sign in with Google — one click, no passwords, no accounts to create

Search — full YouTube catalog, instant results

Home feed — top charts, fresh drops, chill vibes

Your library — playlists pulled straight from your YouTube account

Liked songs — saved locally per account

Queue — add songs, reorder, clear

Full player — glowing album art, dynamic color theming that shifts with each song

Shuffle & repeat — all modes supported

Mini player — always visible at the bottom
with EQ animation

Keyboard shortcuts — Space, arrows, L to like
